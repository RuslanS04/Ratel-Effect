Why The Ratel?

The honey badger (Ratel) is universally recognized as nature's most fearless animal. It attacks lions, raids beehives, devours venomous snakes, and walks away from encounters that would destroy any other creature. It doesn't care about the odds — it fights everything and fears nothing.

RatelEffect channels that raw, untameable energy into the digital realm. Each of the 4,999 NFTs features a unique Ratel character transformed through multiple layers of striking visual effects — from glitch distortions to neon pulses, pixel decimation to chromatic chaos. Just like the real honey badger, every piece is bold, fearless, and absolutely unique.

This isn't just a collection. This is a movement. Holding a RatelEffect means you embody the spirit of relentless courage — the kind that never backs down, never gives up, and never asks for permission.

Everything you need to know about RatelEffect. Can't find what you're looking for? Reach out to us through our community channels.

What is RatelEffect?

+

RatelEffect is a collection of 4,999 unique NFTs featuring honey badger (Ratel) characters transformed through multiple layers of stunning visual effects. Each piece is a one-of-a-kind digital artwork that embodies the fearless, unstoppable spirit of the honey badger — nature's most courageous creature.

How many NFTs are in the collection?

+

The total supply of RatelEffect is 4,999 unique NFTs. Each one features a distinct combination of base character and effect layers, making every single Ratel completely unique.

What makes the art unique?

+

Each RatelEffect NFT starts with a base Ratel character that is then layered with various digital effects — glitch distortions, chromatic shifts, neon pulses, pixel decimation, and many more. The combination of different characters and effect layers creates a truly one-of-a-kind piece of digital art.

What are the mint phases?

+

There are 4 mint phases: Team (reserved for core team), GTD (Guaranteed — for whitelisted members), FCFS (First Come First Serve — for early supporters), and Public (open to everyone). Each phase ensures fair access to the collection based on your involvement level.

Why is the honey badger so significant?

+

The honey badger (Ratel) is listed in the Guinness Book of World Records as the world's most fearless animal. It fights lions, eats venomous snakes, raids beehives, and is virtually indestructible. Its thick skin makes it almost immune to stings, bites, and even machete blows. RatelEffect captures this legendary resilience and fearlessness in digital form.

Where can I trade RatelEffect NFTs?

RatelEffect NFTs can be viewed and traded on OpenSea. Visit our official OpenSea page to browse the collection and make offers on your favorite Ratels.
